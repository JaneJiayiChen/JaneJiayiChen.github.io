[Updated at 2026/01]

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at [Peking University School and Hospital of Stomatology](
https://ss.bjmu.edu.cn/).  My supervisor is Professor [Zhigang Cai](https://ss.bjmu.edu.cn/Html/News/Articles/102059.html), and my co-supervisor is Associate Professor [Jingang An](https://ss.bjmu.edu.cn/Html/News/Articles/199.html). My research focuses on the clinical study of Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw（MRONJ) .

Research Interests
------
- Clinical management of Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw
  - Surgical Treatment Outcomes and Prognostic Factors in Mandibular Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw (Current Research Output)
    - Submitted to: International Journal of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery
    - Current Status: Accepted, pending technical editing
  - Imaging manifestations of Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw (Ongoing Projects)
- Incidence and Risk Factors of Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw in Patients with Malignant Tumors (Ongoing Projects)
